# Research: Local AI, Prediction, And Multimodal Opportunities

Explorer should examine local inference, multimodal generation, and forecasting-oriented tools as possible extension opportunities, compatibility targets, or inspiration for future capability packs.

Focus:
- local or self-hostable capability where possible
- extension-friendly inference and multimodal surfaces
- inspiration for future predictive or creative capability packs

Things to look at:
- Which of these can realistically fit KOTA through adapters or extension protocols?
- Are any of these strong enough to justify future optional capability packs?
- What is worth supporting now versus just tracking as inspiration?
