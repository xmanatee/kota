# Research: Local AI, Prediction, And Multimodal Opportunities

Explorer should examine local inference, multimodal generation, and forecasting-oriented tools as possible extension opportunities or inspiration for future adapters.

Focus:
- local or self-hostable capability where possible
- extension-friendly inference and multimodal surfaces
- inspiration for future predictive or creative capability packs

Questions:
- Which of these can realistically fit KOTA through adapters or extension protocols?
- Are any of these strong enough to justify future optional capability packs?
- What is worth supporting now versus just tracking as inspiration?

Resources:
- https://x.com/anemll/status/2035901335984611412 — X post from `@anemll`; the fetched page was thin, but it appears relevant to local/on-device model execution and should be re-verified directly by Explorer.
- https://github.com/google-research/timesfm — time-series foundation model for forecasting.
- https://github.com/huggingface/transformers.js/releases/tag/4.0.0 — major JS/browser inference release with broader model/runtime support.
- https://github.com/666ghj/MiroFish — swarm-intelligence and prediction engine with knowledge-graph and multi-agent themes.
- https://clawhub.ai/steipete/nano-banana-pro — image generation and editing plugin listing.

Desired outcome:
- recommendations for future local AI, forecasting, or multimodal extension opportunities
- keep this exploratory unless a clean adapter path is obvious
